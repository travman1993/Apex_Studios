Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

What areas do you service?

+

We primarily serve the Cartersville, GA area and surrounding regions, but we're available for projects throughout Georgia and can discuss travel to other locations for specific projects.

How much does a typical project cost?

+

Project costs vary based on the scope, complexity, and specific requirements. We provide custom quotes for each project after understanding your needs. Our starting prices for each service are listed above, and we're happy to work with your budget.

How long does a project typically take?

+

Project timelines depend on the type and scope of work. Drone photography sessions can be completed in a day with deliverables within a week, while web development projects typically take 4-8 weeks from start to finish. App development may take 3-6 months depending on complexity.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance for websites and apps?

+

Yes, we offer maintenance packages to keep your digital assets up-to-date, secure, and performing optimally. These can be customized to your specific needs and budget.

Can you help with content creation for my website or social media?

+

Absolutely! Content creation is one of our specialties. We can create professional photos, videos, and written content tailored to your brand and audience.
